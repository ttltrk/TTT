### Intro

---

```
The man - El hombre
The women - La mujer
The boy -  El nino
The girl - La nina.
A man =  Un hombre
A boy = Un nino
A woman = Una mujer
A girl = Una nina

Yo = I'm
Tu = You
El = He
Ella = She

Yo como, tú comes, él come. = I eat, you eat, he eats.
```
